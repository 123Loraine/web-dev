Recipe for Rock Cakes

    Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Raisings
  • Eggs
  • Vanila Essence
  • Margarine
  • Milk

Method

  1. Seive flour and sugar
  2. Rub in margarine
  3. Add egg, milk and vanila essence
  4. Add raisings
  5. Add milk until its a stiff dough
  6. Spoon into a baking tray at 180 oC

Rock cakes can be served with warm chocolate milk or chilled juice as a dessert

Sticky Chicken Wings

    Ingredients

  • 4 pounds chicken wings
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 6 green onions , chopped, divided
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds

Method

  1. Using a sharp knife, cut through the 2 wing joints; discard wing tips
  2. Place remaining wing pieces in a 4- or 5-qt. slow cooker.
  3. Stir in barbecue sauce, soy sauce and 1/4 cup chopped green onions.
  4. Cook, covered, on high 3-4 hours or until tender.
  5. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and remaining green onions.

    Cranberry Maple Chicken

  • 2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 6 boneless skinless chicken breast halves (4 ounces each)
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup

Method

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the cranberries, water and sugar.
    Cook over medium heat until berries pop, about 15 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper. In a large nonstick skillet, cook 
         chicken in oil over medium heat until juices run clear, 4-5 minutes on each side. 
        Stir syrup into cranberry mixture; serve with chicken.
